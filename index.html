HOW IT WORKS

From story to action.

01

Watch

1–3 minute cinematic vertical episodes. Real actors, professional direction. Characters facing mental health, climate, equity, and civic challenges — written like the shows you already binge.

02

Act

End-card CTAs route you to vetted nonprofit partners. Sign a pledge. Volunteer. Register to vote. Donate. Verified, trackable, optional — never preachy.

03

Unlock

Earn Binge Coins for every verified action. Coins discount your subscription, unlock bonus episodes, and build a private civic record only you can see.

04

Connect

Join show-specific Discord communities. Discuss episodes, share actions taken, and find others who care about the same issues. The story continues off-screen.
