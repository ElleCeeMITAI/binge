HOW IT WORKS

From story to action.

01

Watch

1–3 minute cinematic vertical episodes. Characters tackling real-world issues — in entertaining series crafted to a higher standard, with cinematic production, complex storylines, and genuine stakes.

02

Act

End-card Calls-to-Action connect you to vetted nonprofit partners. Sign a pledge. Volunteer. Register to vote. Verified, trackable, optional — never required.

03

Unlock

ॐ

Earn Binge Karma for every verified action. Binge Karma credits discount your subscription, unlock bonus episodes, and build a private civic record only you can see.

04

Connect

Join show-specific Discord communities. Discuss episodes, share actions taken, and find others who care about the same issues. The story continues off-screen.
